Ride. Respect. Repeat.



Nicycles dreams of helping all communities achieve large numbers of confident bicycle riders. We aim to be built of persons of many backgrounds and identities. Our collective passion for bicycles and community fosters a friendly and supportive bicycling environment that reaches strong and far.


Ride. It's why we're here, and probably why you're here. We want anybody to experience the freedom and empowerment of biking no matter their skill level. Respect. Everyone who attempts to learn to ride a bicycle deserves respect and support, and Nicycles wants to achieve a group that does exactly that. Repeat. Take what you get from the club and share it with others. Teach someone how to fix a flat, show them a new bike trail, or ride with someone who would otherwise be riding alone.

  • Fall 2015

    The Beginning

    We dreamed of an environment where cyclists can feel equally capable, important, and rad. Winter training began in a basement and meetings took place. How would our group conduct itself, develop events and progress? The motto "Ride, Respect, Repeat" was born!

  • January 2016

    Building Trust

    Many people expressed support for Nicycles and our dream. There was a small number of dedicated people that shined. With stable members established, we bonded by joining each other on long rides, brainstorming logos and designing kits. Velodrome racing classes began.

  • Spring 2016

    Business & Bookends

    Hands were being shaken, papers were being autographed, and events were being scheduled. How does a team this small create a ripple so big? The challenges became real and the questions were getting harder.

  • Present & Future

    Living the Dream

    Fix-it clinics, Nicycles events, and community races are just some of the places you can find Nicyclists in the twin cities! Smiles and support from all around.
